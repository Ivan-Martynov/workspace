Semantics

Section

The <section> element is good for grouping relevant information.

Header and Footer

The <header> and <footer> elements are the logical semantic choices for, well, header and footer of the document, respectively. They can also be applied to a section.

Navigation Bar

The <nav> element is for navigation links.

Figure

The <figure> element for illustrations, diagrams and alike.

Description Lists

They are used to represent terms and their respective definitions with the help of <dl>, <dt> and <dd> tags.
